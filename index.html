About

Permitted Produce is one of Ireland's biggest music festival since 2005. Bringing you the best DJs in house and techno from around the world from two days of great music.


Founded by brothers John and Paul, having spent time in the Netherland during the 90s and early 2000s they fell in love with the music culture and they wanted to bring that same culture back home with them to Ireland.
